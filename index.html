In 2018, my journey into coding began, fueled by the nostalgic joy of PS2 classics like Pac-Man World 2 and Gran Turismo. Since then, I've immersed myself in programming fundamentals as part of my computer science coursework, exploring languages such as C, Java, and Python.

Additionally, I'm currently engaged in a language translation project using Next.js.

Away from the keyboard, you'll find me climbing, running, or indulging in my love for retro arcade racers. This blend of technical expertise and real-world interests defines my approach to both coding and life beyond the screen.
